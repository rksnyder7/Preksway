Preksway Eco-Tourism

Preksway offers you the chance to experience Cambodian life in a small fishing village surrounded by stunning nature. Our mission is to teach others to love and care for their natural environment and culture.

Reservation

Conservation

Our conservation efforts are focused on preserving the environment, expanding our Buddhist culture, and creating sustainable local products.

Environment

We strive to help with what we can in the surrounding environment. The biggest problem facing our village is too much plastic use. We need volunteers with curious minds to create new products and ideas that reduce our dependency on plastic. Solar energy is also being researched.

Culture

Our culture is based on thankfulness and gratitude following the Buddha and his philosphy to serve others and play art of privilege culture in our heart.

Local Products

The Preksway community is always looking for new sustainable products to create from local resources. Currenlty, we create sustainable products such as coconut oil, bamboo straws, and grass hats.

Education

Preksway is running a small education center to teach local children english, chinese, and computer science. The children also learn how to communicate with different people from cultures all across the world. In the future, we plan to send some students to study abroad to further their education.

Food

  • Local

    Our food is locally sourced from Preksway farms and the nearby Sihanoukville community.

  • Cooking

    Local teenagers are taught cooking skills through our own Mangrove Cafe & Restaurant which offers tasty Cambodian dishes to visitors.

  • Farming

    Fresh produce is grown through Preksway's own aquaponics farm.

Sports

Preksway locals enjoy playing volleyball and football, and watching boxing. Volunteers are encouraged to participate. Johney plans to open a Marine Kohrong Boxing Club and a Kohrung Football club.s
